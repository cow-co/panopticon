# Panopticon
## An app to allow you to visualise and track your digital footprint

## What is PANOPTICON?

A digital footprint tracker which is:

- Standalone
- Privacy-respecting (I record no telemetry and I do not record any of the personal data you may enter)
- Non-internet-connecting (tested on a disconnected environment)
- Graph-based (with support for basic graph traversal operations)
- Easy-to-use

## What is PANOPTICON *not*?

- Something to search for uses of your information on the internet. 
  - I very much do *not* want your personal info. Not least because that would entail legal obligations I can't be bothered to deal with.
  - There are tools out there which will already do this.
  - I don't think it's massively useful, particularly for very privacy-conscious people, who may not give a full set of identifiers to such a service.
- Something that will allow complex inferences across the graph
  - Too much effort for not enough payoff on that one, I think
  - Though if there's a lot of call for it, I may reconsider
- Something designed to help people on the run from the authorities
  - This shit won't help you if you are.
  - I do not condone illegality, and do not license the use of this tool for such purposes. That is, like, the only license condition.
